Quick and creamy Avocado Pasta that's healthy and vegan. High in fiber, vitamin C, iron and good fats. Easy to make in under 15 minutes.

Ingredients

5 oz whole grain pizza

1 good handful cherry tomatoes

1/4 cup basil leaves (dry basil will do too, but use less)

1 large avocado

2-3 tbsp lemon juice

1-2 tbsp soy sauce

1 clove garlic

Salt to taste

Add a few tbsps of water, if too thick.

Optional

2 tbsp sesame seeds, roasted

1 thumb ginger

Prep Time

15 minutes

Total Time

15 minutes

Servings

2 people

Calories

602 kcal
